IkiReader

What for?

For viewing photos vertically.


For whom?

This project is for manga readers and translators. It is enough for the translator to create an open folder on google drive and copy the link, then paste it into the field below and get a link to the reader. And readers just need to follow the link created by the translator.



Safely?

Yes, you can look at the open source code in your browser. Also, this site does not have a backend, requests are sent directly from your browser to Google servers.
